# Background Music for Videos

Place your background music files (MP3 format) in this directory.

## Recommended Music Moods

### For Funny/Comedy Shorts:
- **upbeat.mp3** - Energetic, bouncy music for funny content
- **suspenseful.mp3** - Build-up music for plot twists
- **playful.mp3** - Light, fun music for humorous moments
- **epic.mp3** - Dramatic music for over-the-top reactions

### For Educational/Inspirational:
- **motivational.mp3** - Uplifting, inspiring background music
- **calm.mp3** - Gentle music for learning content
- **energetic.mp3** - Upbeat music to keep engagement high

## Free Royalty-Free Music Sources

1. **YouTube Audio Library** (Free for any use)
   - https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UC/music
   - No attribution required
   - Wide variety of genres

2. **Pixabay Music** (Free)
   - https://pixabay.com/music/
   - No attribution required
   - High quality tracks

3. **Free Music Archive** (Various licenses)
   - https://freemusicarchive.org/
   - Check individual licenses
   - Large collection

4. **Incompetech** (Free with attribution)
   - https://incompetech.com/music/royalty-free/
   - Kevin MacLeod's music
   - Widely used

5. **Bensound** (Free with attribution)
   - https://www.bensound.com/
   - Professional quality
   - Clean licensing

## Naming Convention

Name your files based on the mood they convey:
- `upbeat.mp3` - Fast, energetic, happy
- `funny.mp3` - Comedic, bouncy, playful
- `suspense.mp3` - Building tension, mysterious
- `chill.mp3` - Relaxed, calm, smooth
- `epic.mp3` - Dramatic, powerful, grand
- `motivational.mp3` - Inspiring, uplifting
- `energetic.mp3` - High energy, exciting

## Tips

1. Keep music files under 3 minutes (they will loop automatically)
2. Use MP3 format for compatibility
3. Normalize audio levels (around -3dB to -6dB peak)
4. Background music will be automatically mixed at 30% volume (adjustable in config.py)
